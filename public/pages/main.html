Why Choose the Lightning Network?

The Lightning Network addresses several critical issues in digital advertising and cryptocurrency transactions:

  • Speed: Traditional payment systems can take time to process transactions. The Lightning Network allows for instant payments, enhancing user experience.
  • Cost Efficiency: With lower transaction fees compared to on-chain transactions, advertisers can maximize their budgets while publishers retain more revenue.
  • Fraud Prevention: The transparent nature of the Lightning Network helps reduce ad fraud by providing verifiable records of impressions and payments.
  • User Empowerment: Users have more control over their transactions, allowing them to engage directly with advertisers without intermediaries.
